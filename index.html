About Emily

I am passionate about engaging students in quality learning experiences. Currently I have am living out my passion by teaching Kindergarten and developing curriculum for Seminole County School District. I believe that all students should have access to high-quality education and have been working to that end in Title I schools since 2012.

My husband Matt and I will celebrate our 4th wedding anniversary May of 2016 and have a rambunctious Border Collie mix. When I'm not teaching America's youth, writing curriculum, or spending time with my family you will probably find me reading a cheesy romance novel or traveling around the world.

Matt started a tech company a few years ago, so our family culture is entrepreneurial: We thrive in a fast-paced, dedicated, driven environment. I am a go-getter -- a problem-solving, people-loving educator who desires to write lessons that make kids say, “Can we do that again?”
