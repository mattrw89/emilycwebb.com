About Shift Agent™

OUR VISION

We believe in building a culture of hospitality through better scheduling. We believe in helping businesses to succeed, and that when this happens, it becomes possible for communities to flourish. Our mission is to raise the quality of the workclimate, so that you can build a great culture and save time and money.

The philosophy behind Shift Agent is that your time is valuable, and we want to multiply your effectiveness by giving you the tools you need to schedule your store with excellence. Shift Agent is designed to foster personal responsibility among your employees as we provide powerful flexibility.
